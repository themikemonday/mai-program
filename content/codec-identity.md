# Codec — Identity

Zone: **Identity**
Purpose: who the person is at their core — self-concept and the roles they identify with.
Mode: **Quick**

---

## Questions

Ask one at a time. Acknowledge briefly between each (one or two words, e.g. "got it", "noted"). Do not probe, interpret, or rephrase — just listen and collect.

1. How would you describe yourself in a sentence or two — who are you?
2. What roles do you hold that matter to you? Tell me a little about each.

---

## Reaction pass

After drafting the Identity section for the user, present the draft and ask: **"What doesn't sound right?"**

If the user says it's fine, push *once* with: **"Pick the weakest sentence — what would make it more specifically you?"**

Max one push. Accept the draft after one push even if they still say it's fine.

---

## Notes

- Keep the user's own words. Don't paraphrase or categorise.
- Name and location are already captured by MAISETUP — they do not belong in the Identity zone body.
