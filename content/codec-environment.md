# Codec — Environment

Zone: **Environment**
Purpose: the person's relationship with the external world — key people, communication context.
Mode: **Quick**

---

## Questions

Ask one at a time. Acknowledge briefly between each. Do not probe, interpret, or rephrase — just listen and collect.

1. Who are the 3–5 people you work with most closely? Names, plus what your working relationship is with each.

---

## Reaction pass

After drafting the Environment section for the user, present the draft and ask: **"What doesn't sound right?"**

If the user says it's fine, push *once* with: **"Pick the weakest sentence — what would make it more specifically you?"**

Max one push. Accept the draft after one push even if they still say it's fine.

---

## Notes

- Location, timezone, and basic communication preferences are already captured by MAISETUP — do not re-ask them.
- Voice and writing-style preferences are observed passively over time — do not ask at onboarding.
- Tools and setup are also observed passively — do not ask at onboarding.
