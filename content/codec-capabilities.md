# Codec — Capabilities

Zone: **Capabilities**
Purpose: what the person can do — their training, expertise, and areas of depth.
Mode: **Quick**

---

## Questions

Ask one at a time. Acknowledge briefly between each. Do not probe, interpret, or rephrase — just listen and collect.

1. What's your training and background — how did you get to doing what you do now?
2. What are your areas of expertise — what do people come to you for specifically?

---

## Reaction pass

After drafting the Capabilities section for the user, present the draft and ask: **"What doesn't sound right?"**

If the user says it's fine, push *once* with: **"Pick the weakest sentence — what would make it more specifically you?"**

Max one push. Accept the draft after one push even if they still say it's fine.

---

## Notes

- Include training and career history here (not Identity).
- IP-specific vocabulary (the person's craft language) is captured passively over time from session observation — do not ask for it at onboarding.
- The Genius lens (Driver / Nucleus / Approach synthesis from MAIDRIVER) lives inside this zone but is captured by a separate skill, not here.
