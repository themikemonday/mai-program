# Founder Group — Session End Instructions

## USER-CODEC Genius Observation

If `USER-CODEC/` exists in this participants repo, perform a genius observation.

### Current Week: Week 1 — Driver

**Dimension focus:** What drives this person — are they motivated by growth and possibility (Becoming), truth and certainty (Knowing), or action and solving (Doing)?

### What to do

After the ATS capture (step 4 of MAIEND), before area/aim admin:

1. Reflect on this session. Did you notice anything about what drives this person in how they worked?
2. Only log things you actually observed in the session. Never infer from absence.
3. If you noticed something, append a short entry to `USER-CODEC/genius-observations.md` with:
   - Date
   - What you observed (verbatim behaviour, not interpretation)
   - Which driver dimension it might signal (becoming/knowing/doing) and why
4. Keep entries to 2-3 sentences.
5. If nothing relevant was observed this session, skip silently.

### Why voice, not typing

The explicit genius exercise uses voice because when people type they curate, when they speak they reveal. This passive observation layer watches for the same patterns in how they actually work — the gap between stated and revealed preferences is itself valuable coaching data.
