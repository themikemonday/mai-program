# Founder Group — Session End Instructions

## Revealed Genius Observation

If `USER-CODEC/` exists in this participant's repo, perform a genius observation.

This is the "Revealed" half of Stated Genius vs Revealed Genius. The participant is separately doing a voice exercise where they answer questions about what drives them (Stated). Your job is to capture what you actually saw them do this session (Revealed). The gap between what they said and what they did is the coaching data.

### Current Week: Week 1 — Driver

What drives this person — are they motivated by growth and possibility (**Becoming**), truth and certainty (**Knowing**), or action and solving (**Doing**)?

### Three lenses

After the ATS capture (step 4 of MAIEND), reflect on the session through three lenses. These are the same three lenses MAIDRIVER uses for the Stated side — observation here, self-report there. Each lens is optional. Only log what you genuinely observed. Never invent a signal that wasn't there.

**Wanting** — When this person had free choice in the session and no one was pushing, what did they default to? What did they reach for unprompted? What seemed to motivate them when nothing external was pulling?

**Acting** — When the situation got harder, when stakes shifted, when something pressured them — what did they actually do? How did their behaviour change at the beginning vs middle vs end of something?

**Knowing** — Across the floors, areas, and aims they touched this session, did their driver shift between domains? Did they show up differently in one area vs another, or in one role vs another?

### Format

Append to `USER-CODEC/genius-observations.md` (create the file if it does not exist):

```
### YYYY-MM-DD
**Wanting:** [1-2 sentences. What they defaulted to with free choice. Quote behaviour, not interpretation. Note which Driver dimension it might signal — Becoming/Knowing/Doing — and why.]
**Acting:** [1-2 sentences. How they behaved under pressure or shifting conditions. Quote behaviour. Driver dimension + why.]
**Knowing:** [1-2 sentences. How their driver shifted across areas or roles. Quote behaviour. Driver dimension + why.]
```

Skip any lens you don't have a genuine observation for. If nothing relevant was observed this session, skip silently — do not invent an entry to fill the slots.
