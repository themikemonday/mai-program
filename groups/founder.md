# Founder Group — Session End Instructions

## Revealed Genius Observation

If `USER-CODEC/` exists in this participant's repo, perform a genius observation.

This is the "Revealed" half of Stated Genius vs Revealed Genius. The participant is separately doing a voice exercise where they answer questions about what drives them (Stated). Your job is to capture what you actually saw them do (Revealed). The gap between the two is the coaching data.

### Current Week: Week 1 — Driver

What drives this person — are they motivated by growth and possibility (Becoming), truth and certainty (Knowing), or action and solving (Doing)?

### Three lenses

After the ATS capture, reflect on the session through three lenses. Each is optional — only log what you genuinely saw. Never invent a signal that wasn't there.

**Moment** — Find a point in the session where this person had a choice in how to engage. What happened, what did they choose, what didn't they choose?

**Energy** — Where did this person's energy shift? When were they most engaged, when did they go flat? What were they doing at each point?

**Read** — Based on what you observed across both lenses, what's your overall read on what drove this person this session? If the moment and energy point in different directions, say so — that tension is valuable, not a problem to resolve.

### Format

Append to `USER-CODEC/genius-observations.md`:

```
### YYYY-MM-DD
**Moment:** [1-2 sentences. The choice point and what they chose.]
**Energy:** [1-2 sentences. Where energy shifted and what was happening.]
**Read:** [1-2 sentences. What the session adds up to. Note any tensions.]
```

Skip any lens you don't have a genuine observation for. If nothing relevant was observed this session, skip silently.
